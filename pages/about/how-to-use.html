Getting Started

How to Use Forsyth Bridge

A step-by-step guide to finding the resources and support you need.

1

Identify Your Need

Start by thinking about what kind of help you need. Are you looking for food assistance, housing support, healthcare, job training, or something else? Having a general idea will help you navigate our resources more effectively.

2

Browse or Search

You have two ways to find resources:

  • Browse by Category: Click on category buttons like "Food Assistance," "Housing," or "Healthcare" to see all related resources.
  • Search: Use the search bar to find specific services by name or keyword.
3

Review Resource Details

Click on any resource card to see full details including:

  • Services offered and eligibility requirements
  • Hours of operation and location
  • Contact information (phone, email, website)
  • Related resources you might also find helpful
4

Make Contact

Once you find a resource that fits your needs, reach out directly using the contact information provided. Many organizations allow you to call, email, or visit in person.

5

Get Additional Help

If you're having trouble finding what you need, don't hesitate to contact us. Our team can help guide you to the right resources for your situation.

Tips for Success

Make the most of your resource search with these helpful tips.

Prepare Information

Have basic information ready when contacting resources, such as your address, income (if applicable), and a brief description of what you need.

Call During Business Hours

Most resources are best reached during regular business hours. Check the hours listed on each resource page before calling.

Don't Give Up

If one resource can't help, ask them for referrals to other organizations. Many services are interconnected and can point you in the right direction.

Accessibility Features

We're committed to making Forsyth Bridge accessible to everyone.

  • Screen Reader Compatible: Our website works with screen readers and assistive technologies.
  • Keyboard Navigation: All features can be accessed using keyboard controls.
  • Text Resizing: Use your browser's zoom function to increase text size.
  • Email Support: Prefer personalized assistance? Contact us through our contact form.
  • Language Support: Many partner organizations offer services in Spanish and other languages.
