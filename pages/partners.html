Join our network of trusted organizations to expand your reach and better serve Forsyth County residents.

Benefits of Partnership

Being a Forsyth Bridge partner means your organization reaches more people who need your services.

Increased Visibility

Get your services in front of thousands of community members actively seeking resources.

Client Referrals

Receive referrals from our resource navigators who connect people with your services.

Network Access

Connect with 200+ other organizations for collaboration and resource sharing.

Event Promotion

Promote your events and programs through our community calendar and newsletter.

Impact Data

Access analytics on how community members are engaging with your listing.

Marketing Support

Get featured in our success stories, social media, and community outreach.

How Partnership Works

Getting started is simple and free for qualifying nonprofits.

1

Apply

Complete our partnership application with your organization details.

2

Review

Our team reviews your application within 5 business days.

3

Onboard

Create your listing and get access to the partner portal.

4

Connect

Start receiving referrals and connecting with community members.

Partner Portal

As a partner, you get access to our self-service portal where you can:

  • Update your organization information and services
  • Manage your hours and contact details
  • Add and promote upcoming events
  • View referral analytics and engagement data
  • Access partner resources and training materials
  • Connect with other partner organizations
