About Forsyth Bridge

Building connections between our community members and the resources they need to thrive.

Our Mission

Forsyth Bridge serves as the central hub connecting individuals and families in Forsyth County to essential resources, support services, and community programs.

We believe that navigating the complex landscape of available services shouldn't be a barrier to getting help. Our platform simplifies this process, making it easier for everyone to find the support they need.

How to Use the Hub

Our Vision

A Forsyth County where every resident has easy access to the support and resources they need to live healthy, stable, and fulfilling lives.

How We Work

Our Story

Forsyth Bridge was founded in 2018 by a group of local nonprofit leaders who recognized the need for a centralized resource hub. What started as a simple spreadsheet shared among organizations has grown into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of county residents each year.

Today, we continue to expand our reach, add new resources, and strengthen our partnerships to better serve Forsyth County.
